About

Advisory depth, not just
compliance work.

Northern Advisory is led by Matthew Etreni, CPA, with seven years spent in private equity and real estate development finance before stepping out to work directly with business owners.

Most accounting providers can keep your books straight and your filings on time. Fewer can also tell you whether a deal pencils out, model a refinance, or build the forecast your lender actually wants to see. That gap between bookkeeping and real financial judgment is what Northern Advisory is built to close.

The practice works with a small, deliberately limited number of business owners at a time, with a particular focus on real estate investment and adjacent sectors.

At a glance

  • CPA, registered with CPA Ontario
  • 7+ years in private equity & real estate development finance
  • Based in Toronto, working with owner-operated businesses
  • Working with small and growing businesses, plus the individuals behind them
  • CSRS 4200-compliant compiled financial statements
